About Me

My projects reflect a balance between analytical thinking and practical application, ranging from business-focused dashboards to NLP-based analysis of real-world data. I approach machine learning as a tool to better interpret data rather than an end in itself.

Currently, I am actively building my skills through hands-on projects, continuous learning, and experimentation, with a focus on developing a strong foundation as a data analyst.
